About

Nine years at the intersection of data engineering and regulatory compliance.

I'm a data and analytics professional with a Master of Science in Industrial & Systems Engineering and nearly a decade of experience supporting insurance and financial systems across the United States. My work centers on the data environments that make actuarial valuation, reserve measurement, claims administration, and enterprise reporting possible — while the organizations that depend on them continue to meet live regulatory obligations.

That work sits inside frameworks shaped by the Financial Accounting Standards Board, statutory principles coordinated through the NAIC, and disclosure standards enforced by the SEC. Modernization in this world can't pause the ledger — it has to happen underneath a system that's still running, still being audited, still serving real people.
